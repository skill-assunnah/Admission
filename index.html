Glossary (Sample)

  • ABS: Anti-lock Braking System — prevents wheel lock during hard braking. (বাংলা: হঠাৎ ব্রেক করলে চাকা লক হওয়া রোধ করে)
  • Alternator: Generates electricity to recharge the battery & power electronics. (ব্যাটারি চার্জ করে)
  • EFI: Electronic Fuel Injection — electronically controlled fuel delivery. (বৈদ্যুতিক নিয়ন্ত্রণে জ্বালানি ইনজেকশন)
  • ECU: Electronic Control Unit — manages engine/electrical functions. (ইঞ্জিন ও ইলেকট্রিক নিয়ন্ত্রণ)

FAQ

Do I need prior experience?

No. The program starts from fundamentals and builds up to diagnostic practice.

What is the weekly routine?

Six days/week (Sat–Thu), 7 sessions/day: 3 theory and 4 practical. See the class routine for timing.

Is there a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of assessments and final exam.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. Limited installment plans are available. See “Fees & Scholarships”.

Is placement support available?

We provide guidance on job readiness, documentation, and interview prep. Local placement support where available.
